Indian Constitution

The Constitution of India is the supreme law of India which was formed on 26th November, 1949. The document lays down the framework that demarcates fundamental political code, structure, procedures, powers, and duties of government institutions and sets out fundamental rights, directive principles, and the duties of citizens.
